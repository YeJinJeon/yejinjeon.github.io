About Me

I am a machine learning research engineer at University of California, San Diego working with Professor Sicun Gao. I received a Master Degree of Computer Science and Engineering with a specialization of AI/ML at UCSD in June, 2024. You can find my Master's thesis here.

My research interest is to develop a general search and optimization algorithm for NP hard problems that tackle safety and fairness on our community.
I am working on imporving LLM saftey by uncovering their vulnerabilities that allow mallicious users to generate harmful content. Specifically, I focus on adversarial prompt generation that can be viewed as combinatorial optimization problem on discrete tokens. Furthermore I develop black box algorithm to general combinatorial optimization problems such as jop shop problem, taking heuristic learning approach with guidance of mixed integer programming solvers and generative model.
Previously, I conducted computer vision research with medical images at Ewha Womans University and Korea University in Korea.

I'm always looking for new opportunities to learn and grow. You can find my full cv here or contact me at yejeon@ucsd.edu.
